A Solid System

Lead generation is simple in theory, but it’s definitely not easy to execute effectively. In fact, only 1 in 10 marketers said that their lead generation efforts are highly efficient and effective.

To ensure the efficiency and consistency of results, we have highly optimized our systems and processes.

01

Goal Setting

Together we define your goals, set targets and plan your campaign. We can advise on how to make your campaign GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation) compliant.

02

Full Target Group Assessment

We research your industry and your prospects’ location, company size, job titles in order to make sure we understand your market correctly.

03

Building a list of prospects

Every prospect is individually checked and added to a database to ensure the highest quality. Their contact information is carefully verified to avoid mistakes.

04

Technical Setup

The setup of your domain, email and DNS as well as the setup of your delivery, open, click and response tracking to assure everything runs smoothly.

05

Fully managed outreach

We combine multiple lead sources, e.g. LinkedIn, BuiltWith, Inc. 5000 or your industry-specific source, with email to create your highly optimized campaign.

06

Review, analyze

Every two weeks we review and analyze the results of your campaign and report them back to you. We keep everything structured together and you can access it at any time.
